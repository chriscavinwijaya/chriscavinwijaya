Short Snippet About Me

Hello there! If it isn’t obvious by now, my name is Chriscavin Wijaya.
Indonesia was where I first got spanked and shed my virgin tears.
That was July 1998. Fast forward several years to December 2013.

Couple months before my GCE ‘O’ Levels, I became a highschool dropout.
High-Risk Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia had decided to waltz into my life.

Flashbacks are cool and all, but that’s that. Now back to the present.

I write, I draw, I animate, I shoot, and I code.
I learn and I create.

However, all these, contrary to everything, is not about me, or at least, not so much.
I created this website to showcase the skills acquired,
as well as the works completed, within my convalescence.

My intentions? To show that obstacles aren’t the end.
That nothing can, nor should, stop your growth.

I hope through this project, with myself as a medium, I can inspire Hope;
Even if just a tiny spark, especially to those struggling with life.

Know that there’ll be light in the darkest times.
It’ll be tough, but you CAN get by, and YOU are worth the struggle.
Don’t let anyone tell you otherwise.
